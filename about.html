Sarah Morrison, Mediator

About Sarah Morrison

Sarah Morrison is a trained mediator and attorney with many years of experience in settlement negotiations. Particular areas of legal knowledge and experience include: litigation, environmental law, consumer laws including the Unfair Competition Law, discrimination and civil rights laws, land use and property law.
